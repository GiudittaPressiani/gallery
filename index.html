ABOUT✨

TOMORROW X TOGETHER is a boyband composed of five members with five different personalities that shine together

Since their debut in 2019 with the song Crown (어느 날 머리에서 뿔이 자랐다) from their first EP The Dream Chapter: STAR, TXT has captured the hearts of fans worldwide

Crown 👑 (어느 날 머리에서 뿔이 자랐다)

TXT's fandom is called MOA, which stands for “Moments of Alwaysness”.

It means that every small moment shared between TXT and fans will always be something eternal. The name also comes from the Korean word moa (모아), meaning “to gather” — symbolizing how fans and TXT complete each other when they come together.

The name was officially revealed on August 22, 2019

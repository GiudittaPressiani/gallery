The Star Chapter: SANCTUARY

It' s the story of beautiful moments spent together - of the person who made each of those experiences magical, and for whom the other chooses to stop running away and start facing reality.

Heaven

This song opens the fourth series, 'Star Chapter'. A pop song with a disco rhythm that stands out for its minimalist instrumentation of piano and synthesizers. A bright and cheerful chord progression is topped with a gentle melody to beautifully express the happiness and joy of reuniting with each other and regaining the starlight. Yeonjun, Soobin, and Taehyun participated in the lyrics.

credits: Melon

The Dream Chapter: Eternity

“Dream Chapter: ETERNITY”, following their debut album “Dream Chapter: STAR” and their first full-length album “Dream Chapter: MAGIC” tells the story of young boys' experiences meeting friends who are different yet alike. While they struggle with reality and rifts sometimes appear among them, the narrative encapsulates their hope that their time together will last eternally. There are six tracks in the album including the opening track “Drama”; title track “Can't You See Me?”; a remake of “Fairy of Shampoo”; first song written by the five members, “Maze in the Mirror”; “PUMA” with unique lyrics; and “Eternally” that exudes an experimental vibe. Highlighting each members' stunning visuals that will capture the hearts of listeners in their teens along with their skills in writing music and lyrics, TOMORROW X TOGETHER's new album is sure to further cement their status as one of today's hottest group.

The popular song 'Shampoo Fairy' by singer 'Light and Salt', which was popular in the 1990s, was reinterpreted in the Dream Pop genre, a subgenre of neo-psychedelics. The fairytale-like lyrics that made him fall in love with the shampoo commercial model on TV, the members' refreshing voices, and a cappella harmonies create a mysterious and dreamy feeling. The warm yet subtle sound of synth instruments creates a unique atmosphere, and the analog sound hidden throughout the song adds to the retro charm.

Maze in the Mirror

This is a Brit Pop genre song co-produced by 'Slow Rabbit' and Beomgyu, the producer of the division. It is a self-composed song written and composed by all five members, led by Yeonjun, and was created before TOMORROW X TOGETHER's debut. She expressed her repetitive daily life during her difficult trainee days in a maze, honestly expressing her desperate desire for someone to find her and her feelings of loneliness. The song progresses with only minimalist instruments of guitar, piano, and bass, as well as the members' emotional and calm vocals, providing a calm impression.

Fairy of Shampoo

A puma who escaped from the zoo. They compared the image of a boy who feels the thrill of encountering a strange world and the freedom he tastes for the first time, but feels fear as he collides with a world that gradually rejects him, to a cougar that has escaped from the zoo. The unique lyrics, captured from the perspective of a fierce beast longing for freedom, stand out. It is a hip hop song based on the trap genre with a strong feel, and although the instrumentation is simple, it conveys explosive energy with just a repetitive rhythm. The hip-hop vibe is strong and heavy, allowing you to feel the expanded musical spectrum of TOMORROW X TOGETHER.

It is a song about boys who chase familiar and unfamiliar longing and search for memories of the past. It contains questions about the mysterious beings and unknown emotions that lead them in repeated nightmares. It is an alternative R&B song that starts with dreamy Acoustic Pop and transitions to a completely different beat with a dark and ominous aura.

Love Language

'Love Language' is a love song that expresses the feelings of studying, deciphering, and learning more about 'you' you love as if learning a new language. 'Love', which anyone in youth can relate to, is solved with intuitive and romantic lyrics.
The song features a bright and upbeat melody in the Afro house genre. They continue the refreshing energy shown in their debut album 'Dream Chapter: STAR', the song 'No Rules' from their second studio album 'Chaos Chapter: FREEZE', and the song 'I'll wait for you tomorrow' from their 6th mini album 'minisode 3: TOMORROW'. The sweet introduction, heart-pounding rhythm, danceable melody, and romantic lyrics featuring Hueningkai are complemented by the members' refreshing voices to maximize the loveliness of the song. This is the birth of TOMORROW X TOGETHER's 'Clear Love Song', which is full of excitement.

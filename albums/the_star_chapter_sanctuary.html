The fairytale-like imagination unique to TOMORROW X TOGETHER, which expresses the reunited 'you' and 'me' as a star waking up, stands out. They talk about a special relationship that brings each other to their comfort zone with just the blink of an eye. It is a medium-tempo R&B song with warm and affectionate emotions, and the combination of rich sound and groovy rhythm is impressive.

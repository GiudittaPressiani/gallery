THURSDAY'S CHILD HAS FAR TO GO

This unit song by Soobin, Beomgyu, and Taehyun is a refreshing and retro synth-pop song. Some of Mother Goose's oral nursery rhymes "Thursday's Child Has Far To Go" and the hashtag "#breakup_glowup", which means taking care of oneself to become a more attractive person instead of being discouraged and collapsing after a breakup, were borrowed as the main themes. By connecting the contradictory themes of oral nursery rhymes and TikTok hashtags, it captures the boy's will to overcome the sadness of separation and move forward on a new path with a positive heart. The catchy retro synth sound, the running beat, and the cool vocals of the three members are combined. Taehyun participated in the composition and lyrics, while Beomgyu participated in producing, adding meaning.

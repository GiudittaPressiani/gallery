THE DREAM CHAPTER:STAR

As they transition from childhood to adolescence, the boys have gathered together under ONE DREAM. "The Dream Chapter" will unfold the stories of what they encountered as they grew. The album "The Dream Chapter: STAR" sings out TOMORROW X TOGETHER's “happiness and excitement of meeting that special someone.” The boys' story opens with 'Blue Orangeade', and closes with 'Nap of a star', narrating how together they make things possible that was impossible alone.

BLUE ORANGEADE

'Blue Orangeade' is a song that freshly interprets the encounter with the opposite person who is too different with the phenomenon of complementary color contrast. New Jack Swing, a genre that was popular from the mid-80s to the early 90s, was made into a trendy yet youthful feel to match the image of TOMORROW X TOGETHER. It is said that it is a special relationship in which we are attracted and attracted to things that we do not have, just like the complementary color relationship between blue and orange.

CROWN

The title track 'One Day I Grew a horn from my head (CROWN)' is a trendy, energetic and modern interpretation of synth pop. The dynamic synth sound and contemporary melody are in perfect harmony. The word 'horn' expresses a kind of growing pains experienced as growing from childhood to boy. Each of the five members, combined with their charming and colorful vocals, unique lyrics, and sophisticated sounds, is expected to meet the expectations and interest of the public.

OUR SUMMER

'Our Summer' is produced by The Futuristics, which has worked with world-class artists such as Halsey, Selena Gomez and Camila Cabello. It is not a fixed season, but a symbolic time or space where you can enjoy friendship and adventure. It is a house-pop genre with a relaxing and sweet melody and a catchy vocal synth theme. If you focus on the lyrics that blend well into the melody, it will be a song that reminds you of your own 'Our Summer' that you experienced at least once someday.

CAT & DOG

I used to be like a cunning and domineering cat, but I am changing because of you. 'Cat & Dog' adds a marimba melody to the trap rhythm to create a cute atmosphere. The song stands out with the members' Mumble Rap, and the narrator sings about the inner changes that occur when meeting someone. The fresh heart that compares the relationship between a pet and a butler, which may be the purest, resembles a scene that anyone would have in their heart. I am surprised and embarrassed by the change I feel for the first time, but the important thing is that I like this change that came one day.

NAP OF A STAR (별의 낮잠)

Nap of the Stars This is a soft modern rock song where you can feel the beautiful harmony of the five members on top of the faint electric guitar playing. Extremely restrained instrument composition and delicate voices of the members. The meaning of stars and naps is a complex meaning of childhood 'dreams', 'anxiety', and 'unattainable ideals', but on the other hand, it also contains the process of forgetting something and overcoming it step by step. The "brilliant night sky" and "magical moment" in the second half of the lyrics are not stories that only appear in songs or fairy tales, but are beautiful moments hidden in the unconscious of all of us. While listening to the song, why not think of your own star that was taking a nap deep in your heart for a while?

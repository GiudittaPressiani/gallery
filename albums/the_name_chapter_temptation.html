The Name Chapter: TEMPTATION

The Name Chapter: TEMPTATION depicts youth on the brink of adulthood. After experiencing conflict and chaos, the boys feel a desire to postpone their growth and linger in the freedom of Neverland. Their journey forward despite this desire is a representation of today's generation as they continue to grow through the challenges of the world.

Farewell, Neverland

It is a rock-based pop genre with concise acoustic guitars and delicate vocals that give a strong sense of immersion. The boy's heart as he accepts the breakup and leaves is expressed with lyrical melodies and sad lyrics. He gives his final goodbyes to Neverland, a beautiful and irresponsible paradise where he succumbed to temptation and set foot on it, and concludes the narrative of 'Name Chapter: TEMPTATION'. Member Yeonjun participated in the lyrics.

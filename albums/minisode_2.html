Minisode 2: Thursday's Child

Gen Z's 'it' boys TOMORROW X TOGETHER continue their journey through the newest EP, minisode 2: Thursday's Child. In minisode 2: Thursday's Child, the experience of a first breakup strikes as a powerful storm mixed with denial, dejection, anger, and loss. Our protagonist fully indulges in and expresses each of these emotions as they come, until he is finally able to accept that his once treasured relationship has come to an end. Moving from adolescence to adulthood, the boy comes to better understand both himself and the world around him. Then, a question arises: on what path of growth will his feet lead him next? Minisode 2: Thursday's Child is a post-farewell recollection with five tracks total: “Good Boy Gone Bad,” “Trust Fund Baby,” “Opening Sequence,” and TOMORROW X TOGETHER's first two unit tracks, “Lonely Boy” and “Thursday's Child Has Far To Go.” The members have made creative contributions to all tracks in the EP.

-blood flows through and from the heart, but since he's heartbroken, he's angry and he doesn't care if he seems heartless
-"killed it, I killed it myself" means "I killed my old self, myself"
-diamond is the hardest material on Earth, in saying that he no longer bleeds blood but instead bleeds diamonds, he's implying that he's turning cold and heartless
-"monochrome" is often used to describe something that has no life or something that is not exiting.

credits: @translatingTXT

LINE 3: "even if I crash down head first with broken wings, it doesn't hurt anymore"
lyric from lo$er=lo♡er: “jump off from this highway, these wings that broke while being spread out,no matter how much i try, i crash. (but) if I'm with you, even crashing is beautiful on the other side of the sky, I'll gladly sink down”

@TXTtranslation'd like to think that this is a heavy reference to the fall of icarus. Icarus was the son of a craftsman, daedalus. daedalus created a labyrinth so that the minator (half human, half bull-monster) would never escape but the king that ordered daedalus to do so, said that daedalus and icarus would be locked in the tower to keep the existence of the labyrinth a secret. Daedalus glued several feathers of birds together with wax to make wings for icarus and himself.

He warned icarus to not fly too close to the sun because the wax would melt off and he'd crash into the sea. He also warned him to not fly too close to the sea because the spray from the sea water would weigh his wings down, also leading to him eventually crashing.

Icarus felt a lot of excitement and thrill from flying for the first time and forgot about the warnings. He flew too high and close to the sun and he crashed into the sea and drowned in it.

Back to the song, like icarus, he was also looking for freedom: the wings that were already broken felt like they didn't matter because the first love felt so exciting that he thought everything was beautiful, even crashing into the sea, as long as he's with his lover. However, just like icarus, he couldn't find the balance between two things & after the heartbreak that this person caused him, even crashing head-first didn't hurt him anymore.

In astronomy, icarus is an asteroid whose orbit brings it closer to the sun than any other known orbit.개나 줘" (give it to the dogs) actually means "I don't need it anymore so even if you give it to me, I won't take it from you"

THURSDAY'S CHILD HAS FAR TO GO

This unit song by Soobin, Beomgyu, and Taehyun is a refreshing and retro synth-pop song. Some of Mother Goose's oral nursery rhymes "Thursday's Child Has Far To Go" and the hashtag "#breakup_glowup", which means taking care of oneself to become a more attractive person instead of being discouraged and collapsing after a breakup, were borrowed as the main themes. By connecting the contradictory themes of oral nursery rhymes and TikTok hashtags, it captures the boy's will to overcome the sadness of separation and move forward on a new path with a positive heart. The catchy retro synth sound, the running beat, and the cool vocals of the three members are combined. Taehyun participated in the composition and lyrics, while Beomgyu participated in producing, adding meaning.
